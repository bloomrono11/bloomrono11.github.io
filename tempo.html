Cover Letter

Why Tempo

To the Tempo Engineering Team,

Over 15+ years I've built production software across fintech, healthtech, travel, and SaaS — always as a full-stack engineer who cares as much about the experience a user gets as the architecture beneath it. When I read about Tempo's mission to make workflows genuinely better and saw the focus on Custom Charts for Jira and Confluence, it resonated directly with the kind of product work I find most meaningful: tools that enterprise teams depend on daily, where reliability, performance, and usability all matter.

On the backend, I'm on solid ground. At Synechron (embedded at Citi), I've been owning high-throughput Java/Spring Boot services — Kafka-based event pipelines with partition design, retry-with-backoff, and CDC patterns; REST APIs secured with JWT; and real-time trade risk integrations under strict SLA requirements. I've championed quality gates through SonarQube and Snyk in CI/CD pipelines, and collaborated with quant teams where getting it right is non-negotiable.

On the frontend, my production experience is with TypeScript and Angular. I've built data-rich dashboards and complex UI workflows — trade monitoring views, dealership onboarding portals, billing interfaces — and I understand the craft of building interfaces that enterprise users trust. The move to React would be an adjustment I'd approach deliberately and quickly, drawing on the same reactive and component-based foundations I already work with daily.

What excites me about Tempo specifically is the Atlassian ecosystem context. I've worked with Jira and Confluence across virtually every team I've been part of, and I understand what makes a plugin feel native versus bolted on. The challenge of building chart visualizations that feel first-class inside Jira — performant, accessible, and trustworthy to enterprise users — is exactly the kind of problem I'd want to own end-to-end.

I thrive in remote, async environments, am comfortable driving technical decisions without hand-holding, and bring a genuine product sensibility alongside engineering depth. I'd welcome the opportunity to talk through how my background maps to what you're building.
